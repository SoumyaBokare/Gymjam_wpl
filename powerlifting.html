Squat Exercises

  • Back Squat:
    • Targets: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, lower back.
    • Effectiveness: Fundamental lift for building overall leg strength and mass.
  • Front Squat:
    • Targets: Quadriceps, core.
    • Effectiveness: Emphasizes the quadriceps and core stability.
  • Box Squat:
    • Targets: Quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings.
    • Effectiveness: Improves squat form and explosiveness.

Bench Press Exercises

  • Flat Bench Press:
    • Targets: Chest, triceps, shoulders.
    • Effectiveness: Essential lift for upper body strength and mass.
  • Incline Bench Press:
    • Targets: Upper chest, shoulders.
    • Effectiveness: Focuses on the upper chest and shoulder strength.
  • Close-Grip Bench Press:
    • Targets: Triceps, chest.
    • Effectiveness: Enhances triceps strength and lockout power.

Deadlift Exercises

  • Conventional Deadlift:
    • Targets: Entire posterior chain, including back, glutes, hamstrings.
    • Effectiveness: Key lift for overall strength and development.
  • Sumo Deadlift:
    • Targets: Glutes, hamstrings, lower back.
    • Effectiveness: Reduces strain on the lower back and emphasizes the glutes and hamstrings.
  • Romanian Deadlift:
    • Targets: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back.
    • Effectiveness: Builds hamstring and glute strength while improving mobility.

Accessory Exercises

Upper Body

  • Overhead Press:
    • Targets: Shoulders, triceps.
    • Effectiveness: Builds shoulder size and strength.
  • Pull-Ups:
    • Targets: Lats, upper back.
    • Effectiveness: Builds width in the back and improves grip strength.
  • Barbell Row:
    • Targets: Middle back, lats, traps.
    • Effectiveness: Builds thickness in the back and enhances posture.

Lower Body

  • Leg Press:
    • Targets: Quadriceps, glutes.
    • Effectiveness: Provides a controlled way to build leg muscle mass.
  • Lunges:
    • Targets: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes.
    • Effectiveness: Improves balance and unilateral leg strength.
  • Calf Raises:
    • Targets: Calves.
    • Effectiveness: Builds calf strength and size.

Core Exercises

  • Plank:
    • Targets: Entire core, stabilizer muscles.
    • Effectiveness: Builds core strength and endurance.
  • Russian Twists:
    • Targets: Obliques, core.
    • Effectiveness: Engages the entire core and improves rotational strength.
  • Leg Raises:
    • Targets: Lower abs.
    • Effectiveness: Strengthens and defines the lower abdominal muscles.
