Cardio & Endurance

  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT):
    • Targets: Cardiovascular system, lungs, legs.
    • Effectiveness: Improves aerobic and anaerobic endurance, builds stamina, and boosts overall cardiovascular health, essential for sustained cycling performance.
  • Steady-State Cycling:
    • Targets: Cardiovascular system, legs.
    • Effectiveness: Enhances endurance for long rides by building aerobic capacity and muscular endurance through sustained moderate-intensity cycling sessions.
  • Tempo Rides:
    • Targets: Cardiovascular system, legs, core.
    • Effectiveness: Increases lactate threshold and improves the bodyâ€™s ability to sustain moderate to high levels of exertion over longer distances.
  • Hill Repeats:
    • Targets: Legs, cardiovascular system.
    • Effectiveness: Builds leg strength, stamina, and endurance by repeatedly climbing hills, simulating real-world cycling challenges.
  • Long-Distance Rides:
    • Targets: Cardiovascular system, legs, core.
    • Effectiveness: Improves endurance and mental toughness, allowing cyclists to maintain performance over extended periods.
  • Fartlek Training:
    • Targets: Cardiovascular system, legs.
    • Effectiveness: Combines intervals of high and low intensity, mimicking the variable pace of outdoor cycling and improving overall cardiovascular fitness.
  • Cross-Training (Running/Swimming):
    • Targets: Cardiovascular system, entire body.
    • Effectiveness: Enhances cardiovascular endurance, provides variety, and helps prevent overuse injuries by incorporating different forms of cardio.
