Meditation and Yoga Exercises

Meditation

  • Mindfulness Meditation:
    • Targets: Mental clarity, focus, relaxation.
    • Effectiveness: Reduces stress, improves concentration and emotional balance.
  • Body Scan Meditation:
    • Targets: Awareness of body sensations, relaxation.
    • Effectiveness: Improves body awareness and stress reduction.
  • Breathing Meditation:
    • Targets: Relaxation, calming the mind.
    • Effectiveness: Helps in reducing anxiety and enhances focus.

Yoga

  • Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana):
    • Targets: Full body stretch, strengthens arms and legs.
    • Effectiveness: Improves flexibility and energizes the body.
  • Child's Pose (Balasana):
    • Targets: Back, hips, thighs.
    • Effectiveness: Relieves stress, stretches the lower back.
  • Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II):
    • Targets: Legs, hips, chest.
    • Effectiveness: Builds strength, stability, and endurance.
  • Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana):
    • Targets: Back, shoulders, chest.
    • Effectiveness: Strengthens the spine and improves posture.
