Chest Exercises

  • Flat Barbell Bench Press:
    • Targets: Mid chest (pectorals), triceps, shoulders.
    • Effectiveness: Builds overall chest size and strength.
  • Incline Dumbbell Press:
    • Targets: Upper chest, shoulders.
    • Effectiveness: Emphasizes upper chest for balanced development.
  • Chest Dips:
    • Targets: Lower chest, triceps, shoulders.
    • Effectiveness: Great for developing the lower portion of the chest.

Back Exercises

  • Deadlift:
    • Targets: Entire back, glutes, hamstrings.
    • Effectiveness: Best compound lift for overall strength and development.
  • Pull-Ups:
    • Targets: Lats, upper back.
    • Effectiveness: Builds width in the back and improves grip strength.
  • Barbell Row:
    • Targets: Middle back, lats, traps.
    • Effectiveness: Builds thickness in the back and enhances posture.

Shoulder Exercises

  • Front Delts - Overhead Press:
    • Targets: Front deltoids, triceps.
    • Effectiveness: Builds shoulder size and strength.
  • Lateral Delts - Lateral Raise:
    • Targets: Lateral deltoids.
    • Effectiveness: Increases shoulder width and definition.
  • Rear Delts - Rear Delt Fly:
    • Targets: Rear deltoids, upper back.
    • Effectiveness: Strengthens the rear delts and improves shoulder stability.

Leg Exercises

  • Squats:
    • Targets: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes.
    • Effectiveness: Best overall exercise for building leg strength and mass.
  • Leg Press:
    • Targets: Quadriceps, glutes.
    • Effectiveness: Provides a controlled way to build leg muscle mass.
  • Romanian Deadlift:
    • Targets: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back.
    • Effectiveness: Builds hamstring and glute strength while improving mobility.

Arm Exercises

Biceps

  • Barbell Curl:
    • Targets: Biceps.
    • Effectiveness: Builds mass and strength in the biceps.
  • Concentration Curl:
    • Targets: Biceps peak.
    • Effectiveness: Isolates the biceps for maximum engagement.

Triceps

  • Skull Crushers:
    • Targets: Triceps.
    • Effectiveness: Isolates and builds triceps size and strength.
  • Triceps Dips:
    • Targets: Triceps, shoulders.
    • Effectiveness: Strengthens and develops the triceps.

Forearm Exercises

  • Wrist Curls:
    • Targets: Forearms (flexors).
    • Effectiveness: Builds forearm strength and size.
  • Reverse Barbell Curl:
    • Targets: Forearms (extensors), biceps.
    • Effectiveness: Strengthens forearms and improves grip.

Core Exercises

  • Plank:
    • Targets: Entire core, stabilizer muscles.
    • Effectiveness: Builds core strength and endurance.
  • Russian Twists:
    • Targets: Obliques, core.
    • Effectiveness: Engages the entire core and improves rotational strength.
  • Leg Raises:
    • Targets: Lower abs.
    • Effectiveness: Strengthens and defines the lower abdominal muscles.

Neck Exercises

  • Neck Flexion (With Resistance Band):
    • Targets: Neck flexors.
    • Effectiveness: Strengthens the front part of the neck.
  • Neck Extension (With Resistance Band):
    • Targets: Neck extensors.
    • Effectiveness: Builds strength in the back of the neck and stabilizes it.
